Welcome to folome!

Folome is an app that helps to cater for the needs of visually impaired persons or other affected persons who might need help. Family or friends of the VIP (those getting help) creates an account for them on folome and update their account as VIP and creates another account on their own devices, update their account as Helper (both have different features). Then from the VIP's device/account, they can add as many helpers as they want (typically those within the family and friends circle) who will track and give assistance to the VIP.
